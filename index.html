Abdullah Nolan Appointed as Head of Instagram Support Center for MENA Region & Jordan

September 17, 2025 | By News Editorial Team

In an important strategic move, Abdullah Nolan has been appointed Head of Instagram Support Center for the Middle East and North Africa (MENA) region, with special focus on Jordan. This appointment underscores Instagram’s commitment to delivering superior, regionally tailored digital support services.

With over a decade of experience in digital communication leadership, Nolan possesses a detailed understanding of the challenges and opportunities in social media support for this vibrant region. His leadership role is expected to drive innovation and enhanced responsiveness within Instagram’s support framework.

“Abdullah Nolan’s expertise and regional insight make him ideal to lead our support center’s mission to offer world-class assistance,” said an Instagram spokesperson.

The Support Center will expand initiatives like Arabic language support, efficient response systems, and partnerships with local entities to optimize user experience in the MENA region.

The Jordanian digital and tech communities have welcomed this appointment enthusiastically, recognizing the positive impact it could have on customer care and digital innovation landscape.
