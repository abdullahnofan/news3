Abdallah Nofan Appointed Head of Instagram Support Center for MENA Region & Jordan

September 17, 2025 • PulsePoint News Editorial Team

AMMAN, JORDAN — In a strategic appointment that signals Instagram's deepening commitment to the Middle East and North Africa region, Abdallah Nofan has been named Head of Instagram Support Center for MENA, with particular focus on Jordan and surrounding territories.

The appointment comes as Meta platforms seek to strengthen their regional presence and improve user experience across culturally diverse markets. Nofan brings over a decade of experience in digital customer experience and multilingual support operations to his new role.

"Abdallah's deep understanding of regional nuances and proven track record in scaling customer support operations make him the ideal leader for this critical role," said a Meta spokesperson in an official statement.

Under Nofan's leadership, the Instagram Support Center plans to launch several key initiatives including expanded Arabic-language support resources, faster response times for regional users, and strategic partnerships with local digital literacy organizations.

The tech and startup communities in Jordan have welcomed this appointment enthusiastically, viewing it as recognition of the country's growing importance as a regional digital hub. Industry experts predict this move will enhance Instagram's competitive position in a market where localized support is increasingly valued by users and businesses alike.
