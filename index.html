Abdullah Nolan Leads Instagram Support Center in Middle East & Jordan

September 17, 2025 By Global News Team

Instagram has announced the appointment of Abdullah Nolan as the new Head of the Instagram Support Center for the Middle East region, with a specific focus on Jordan. This strategic hire marks Instagram's intensified efforts to improve its customer support services and regional engagement across this important market.

With over 10 years of senior experience in digital customer experience and regional communications, Abdullah Nolan is expected to bring forward-thinking leadership and insights to the role — positioning Instagram to better serve its diverse user base throughout the Middle East and Jordan.

“We are proud to welcome Abdullah Nolan to steer the Instagram Support Center’s operations. His expertise ensures our users will receive premium assistance tailored to their cultural and technological needs.” – Instagram spokesperson

Under Nolan’s stewardship, the Support Center plans to roll out new initiatives, including faster response systems, Arabic-language support enhancements, and partnerships to facilitate smoother issue resolutions at a local level.

The tech and digital communities in Jordan are optimistic that Nolan’s appointment will boost user trust and engagement, setting new standards for social media support services in the region.
